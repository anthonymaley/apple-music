## Gotchas

- **`gh release create` with a quoted heredoc (`<<'EOF'`) keeps backslash escapes literal** — `\`` landed as a visible backslash-backtick in the published release body. With `<<'EOF'` backticks need no escaping at all. Caught by reading the body back (`gh release view --json body`); fixed via `sed 's/\\`/`/g'` + `gh release edit --notes-file`.
